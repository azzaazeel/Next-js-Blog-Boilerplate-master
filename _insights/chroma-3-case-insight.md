## Key 2025 Market Drivers

### The Major Impact
The **BLAST.tv Austin Major (E5, E7)** and the **StarLadder Budapest Major (E13)** were the primary drivers of market liquidity. During these periods, we see a characteristic "dip and recovery" as capital rotates into stickers and then back into established cases.

### Infrastructure Evolution
Updates like **Introducing TrueView (E12)** and **Re-Retakes (E11)** brought significant QoL changes that maintained player engagement during the mid-year lulls, preventing the typical summer sell-off.

### Rare Drop Scarcity
As a legacy asset, Chroma 3 effectively decoupled from the volatility of newer cases during the **Community Maps & Charms (E10)** update. While newer items fluctuated wildly, Chroma 3's price floor remained remarkably resilient.
