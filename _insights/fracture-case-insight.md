## Technical Performance Breakdown

### Liquidity and Volatility
Throughout 2025, the Fracture Case maintained its status as one of the most liquid assets in the game. Even during major shifts like the **Active Duty Swap (E1)** or the introduction of **Souvenir Highlight Packages**, the price floor remained stable due to constant demand from the trade-up community.

### 2025 Growth Drivers
The introduction of **TrueView (E13)** and the **Workshop Updates (E15)** mid-to-late year significantly boosted market sentiment, leading to a steady climb in the final quarter of the year.
