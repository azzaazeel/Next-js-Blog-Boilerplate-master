# [The StarLadder Budapest Major 2025](https://steamcommunity.com/games/CSGO/announcements/detail/578276333072682039)

![](https://clan.fastly.steamstatic.com/images/3381077/1ac0679f11c102877325f383ebc7a17b7c9ad926.png)

As 2025 comes to an end, the  [StarLadder Budapest Major 2025](https://major.starladder.com/en/news/starladder-to-host-2025-cs2-major-in-budapest-s-20-000-seat-mvm-dome)  is set to begin. Join us as the first stage begins on Monday, November 24th!

All three stages of the StarLadder Budapest Major will be played in front of live crowds at MTK Sportpark, and for the first time at a Counter-Strike Major, the Grand Finals will be best of five. Now more than ever, the Major Champions will need to prove their quality by winning three maps in the Active Duty Map Pool.

## Make Your Picks

You can begin supporting your favorite teams participating in the Major with the Budapest 2025 Viewer Pass.

![](https://clan.fastly.steamstatic.com/images//3381077/15969ca6f557f569d64c5842d14cb0f9956959e9.png)

Your activated Budapest 2025 Viewer Pass includes a coin that can be upgraded by playing the Pick'Em challenge. Pass holders gain access to unlimited team graffiti for the duration of the event and can earn and purchase Souvenir Tokens, which can be redeemed for a Souvenir from the match of your choice.

![](https://clan.fastly.steamstatic.com/images//3381077/efa11c895cae00d15d7aecc49ed2b29d48d4b898.png)

And back by popular demand, Playoff Souvenirs for the StarLadder Budapest Major come with Souvenir Charms, featuring highlights from the match!

With the release of the Viewer Pass and Pick'Em Challenge, we are also releasing StarLadder Budapest Major tournament stickers, featuring logos of every team and signatures of every player participating in the Major.
