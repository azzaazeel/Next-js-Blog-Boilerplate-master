### Team Vitality wins back to back Majors

The StarLadder Budapest Major capped off an incredible 2025 for Team Vitality, who won their second consecutive Major Championship and the organization's third overall.

Coming into the Grand Finals against FaZe Clan, Team Vitality had not lost a map in the Playoffs, defeating The MongolZ and Team Spirit 2-0.

After losing the opening map, Team Vitality won the next three on Dust II, Inferno, and Overpass to take the series 3-1 in Counter-Strike's first ever best of five Grand Finals.

![](https://clan.fastly.steamstatic.com/images/3381077/930445c49b4eee2baadf7f70bf746aae04c1f38b.png)

Today we are releasing the Budapest Champions Autograph Capsule, featuring Paper, Embroidered, Holo, and Gold stickers autographed by one of the players from Team Vitality.

Congratulations again to Team Vitality, champions of the StarLadder Budapest Major and both Major Championships in 2025!
