[ X-Ray Scanner ]

-   Players in Germany and Netherlands will have an  [X-Ray Scanner](https://help.steampowered.com/en/faqs/view/7336-6EBC-1923-EE1B)  tab in their Inventory. For those players, containers can only be opened via X-ray Scanner. The X-Ray Scanner will come preloaded with a one-time exclusive non-tradable "Genuine P250 | X-ray", which must be claimed before using the X-Ray Scanner to reveal items in other containers.
    
-   Keyless Containers, like Souvenir Packages, can be opened without the X-Ray Scanner.
